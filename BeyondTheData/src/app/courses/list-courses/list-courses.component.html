If you find that a particular word or two appear in the output, but are not of value to your particular analysis. You can remove them, specifically, from the text. docs <- tm_map(docs, removeWords, c("department", "email")) # Just replace "department" and "email" with words that you would like to remove. Removing common word endings (e.g., “ing”, “es”, “s”) This is referred to as “stemming” documents. We stem the documents so that a word will be recognizable to the computer, despite whether or not it may have a variety of possible endings in the original text. library(SnowballC) docs <- tm_map(docs, stemDocument)
